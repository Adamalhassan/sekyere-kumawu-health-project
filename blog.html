DHMT Meeting Held at Sekyere Kumawu District Health Directorate

Date: 24th June 2025

Earlier today, the Sekyere Kumawu District Health Directorate successfully held a Larger District Health Management Team (DHMT) meeting at the directorate's conference room. The meeting brought together heads of health facilities and key staff of the district directorate to deliberate on pressing health issues and strategies for improving service delivery across the district.

Key discussions focused on:

  • ✅ Review of district health performance indicators
  • ✅ Strengthening supportive supervision and monitoring
  • ✅ Addressing logistics and supply chain challenges
  • ✅ Improving data quality and timely reporting
  • ✅ Promotion of staff and welfare
  • ✅ Enhancing community engagement for better health outcomes
  • ✅ Staff reshuffle and new role assignments
  • ✅ Staff appraisal processes and timelines
  • ✅ Preparation for the upcoming Half-Year Performance Review

The District Director of Health Services commended all participants for their dedication and encouraged teamwork and commitment towards achieving better health outcomes for the people of Sekyere Kumawu.

Together, we are building a stronger health system!
